# Initial Concept
Bazaar Companion: A specialized analytics dashboard and market tracking tool for the Hypixel Minecraft Bazaar.

# Product Guide: Bazaar Companion

**Vision:**
Empower Hypixel SkyBlock traders with high-performance, real-time market analytics and data-driven insights. By bridging the gap between raw Hypixel Bazaar data and actionable trading strategies, Bazaar Companion aims to be the essential tool for both casual flippers and whale-tier market manipulators.

**Core Objectives:**
1. **Precision Market Tracking:** Deliver real-time OHLC price tracking and historical candle data with zero-latency updates.
2. **Actionable Analytics:** Provide advanced scoring mechanisms for trading opportunities, manipulation detection, and trend analysis.
3. **Immersive UI/UX:** Offer a modern, high-density dashboard that presents complex order book data and market indices in an intuitive, visual format.
4. **Resilient Data Aggregation:** Maintain a high-availability background service to capture and aggregate market ticks into consistent historical intervals.

**Key Features:**
- **Market Dashboard:** A central hub for monitoring overall market trends, top gainers/losers, and key metrics.
- **Advanced Product Analysis:** Deep-dive pages for individual products featuring interactive charts, order book heatmaps, and imbalance gauges.
- **Market Indices:** Aggregated price tracking for themed baskets of items (e.g., Minions, Farming, Slayers).
- **Trading Desk:** Specialized tools for rapid trade calculation and multi-product comparison.
- **Real-time SignalR Updates:** Instant propagation of market ticks from the Hypixel API to the frontend.
- **Whale Order Tracking:** Detection and notification of significant order book activity.

**Target Users:**
- **Casual Traders:** Users looking for simple flipping opportunities and price trends.
- **Professional Market Makers:** Advanced users requiring deep order book analysis and manipulation scoring.
- **Data Analysts:** Users interested in historical market correlations and long-term price trends.
